FAQs
What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects take 4-12 weeks depending on complexity. A basic MVP can be delivered in 4-6 weeks, while full-scale applications with AI integration typically take 8-12 weeks. I provide detailed timelines after our initial discovery call.

Do you handle both frontend and backend development?

Yes, I'm a full-stack developer specializing in end-to-end solutions. I handle everything from backend APIs (Node.js, Laravel, Python) and databases to frontend interfaces (React, Next.js) and mobile apps (Flutter, React Native), including cloud deployment.

Can you integrate AI features into existing applications?

Absolutely. I specialize in OpenAI integrations including chat systems, automated summarization, intelligent workflows, and custom AI features. I can enhance your existing platform with AI capabilities while ensuring proper cost controls and safety measures.

What about ongoing maintenance and support?

I offer flexible support packages including bug fixes, feature updates, performance monitoring, and security patches. All projects include detailed documentation and I provide knowledge transfer to your team if needed.

What's your approach to scalability and performance?

I architect systems with scalability in mind from day one - using microservices, efficient database design, caching strategies, and cloud-native solutions. I implement CI/CD pipelines, monitoring, and performance optimization to ensure your application grows with your user base.
